ABOUT US

Revise started 5 years ago when Berit decided that the amount of fashion offer online was way too big. The search for the perfect winter jacket could take days because there is just so much to compare. On top of that, everything that is nice, is expensive. That is when Revise came to life. Revise will save your closet (and your finances). We are always here to inspire you and to make sure you always feel your best. Beautiful and sophisticated fashion should be available to everyone, everywhere and for every budget. On REVISE.com you will find all the fashion trends for the coming season accompanied with budget friendly fashion tips. We are here for everyone who struggles to find their way through the big hole that we call internet. It can be hard at times to keep on track with the big world of fast fashion and the prices that keep going up. That is why we decided to help you with that and take the search out of your hands.
